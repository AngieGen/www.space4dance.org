JUDGING USING THE OLYMPIC SCORING SYSTEM: OLİMPİK PUANLAMA SİSTEMİNİ KULLANARAK DEĞERLENDİRME:

Using the Olympic scoring systems; one highest and one lowest score are eliminated, the points awarded for each dance will be the aggregate of the remaining three scores .

Each Judge evaluates and scores each performance with a maximum of 100 points as follows:
