Scoring Criteria

Olympic Scoring System

Using the Olympic scoring system where one highest and one lowest scores are eliminated, the points awarded for each dance will be the aggregate of the remaining three scores.

The Venue

X-Belek Hotel

Antalya, Turkey

5-Star Ultra All Inclusive Experience

Fancy a juicy steak? Yep, it's right here. Craving some Sushi? You'll find that too. Or fancy a kid-free date night? There's an adults-only restaurant for some good old couples time.

Pools because we all need options, you've got an aqua park for the kids or an adults-only pool where having a cocktail in hand is mandatory!

Cocktail games, poolside competitions, and by evening the X Stage Shows will blow you away, world-class, famous DJs. The X Belek is definitely a winner!

Location

Just a speedy 5 minutes bus ride to Kadriye Beach - a shuttle service is provided every half an hour!

A 27-minute bus journey from Antalya Airport - so, you're 27-minutes from a cocktail by the pool...

Thrill seekers, listen up! The Land of Legends Theme Park is just a quick taxi ride away (extra charge).

Rooms

Rooms are split into Family Rooms and Adults Only Rooms, all the rooms are spacious, chic and full of amenities (including a full mini-bar with all the holiday essentials, we're looking at you beer)

Standard Rooms, Standard Adults Room (nothing standard about these fyi!), Junior Adults Suite, Swim-Up Adults Room, Family Rooms and Suites

Food & Drink

Two Main Restaurants - two packed buffet restaurants with food stations and show-cooking, the second is for Adults Only.

Snack Restaurant - full of all the holiday goodies, we're talking pizza, burgers and hot dogs... all the naughty stuff.

Beach Snack Bar - your beach day just got better with a selection of fast food bites and delicious ice cream.

Pool Snack Bar - poolside snacks all day long, will you ever go hungry here? Absolutely not.

Patisserie - the kids will be nagging to come here, it's cakes, pastries, cookies, all the sugar galore. We won't tell the dentist if you don't!

Seven Bars for caffeine fixes, holiday cocktails, ice-cold beer, a bit of vino, or a classic Fanta Lemon, you'll find it all here. You can even grab your normal Starbucks order!

Sports & Entertainment

Seven swimming pools - because we all need options, right? A pool for the active swimmers, pools for the kiddos to make as much splash as they want and an adults pool for a peaceful swim or little afternoon snooze

Aquapark - not just for the kids, there's exhilarating slides for the grown-ups too, with a racer slide, and freefall slides that'll make your stomach drop

Fitness centre - very high-tech so if you're gonna do a workout anywhere, it needs to be here! If you need a bit of motivation, there are fitness trainers that'll give you a personalised workout

Spa - that'll give you that 'ooooo' 'aaahhhh' feelin', pamper-yourself-silly with a trip to the sauna, steam room and Turkish Baths, with a massage or skin care treatment to finish

Entertainment

If you're feeling active you've got Pilates, aerobics, Zumba and gymnastics

Get stuck into the pool games, cocktail games, bingo and quizzes

Evening shows four times a week, perfect for the whole family to sit and enjoy

X Planet Mini Club - offering a fantastic entertainment schedule for ages 1 - 8, after a day of fun the youngsters can head back at 20:30 for the nightly disco

X Stage Shows - not your regular bingo, this is next-level entertainment, with world-class DJs, and famous singers taking to the stage, Clean Bandit took to the stage in July (yep, they aren't messing around)
