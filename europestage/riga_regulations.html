Olympic Scoring System

Using the Olympic scoring system where one highest and one lowest score are eliminated, the points awarded for each dance will be the aggregate of the remaining three scores.

Awards Structure

Winning Performances (1, 2 and 3 places) awarded with Diplomas and Trophies

Performances are scored within their own declared nomination: Age Group and Category

Placement Rules:

Age Groups 3-4; 5-6; 7-9 and 10-12: Places can be duplicated in each Age Group and Category.

Age Groups 13-15; 16-18 and Adults: First three places only (i.e. 1st, 2nd and 3rd) DO NOT GET DUPLICATED within the same Age Group and Category, except for:

  • In case of a tie (that is when the same number of points are awarded)
  • In case of 1 point separating places (working from top going down)
