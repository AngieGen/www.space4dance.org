JUDGING & AWARDS/ OLYMPIC SCORING SYSTEM

Each Judge evaluates and scores each performance with a maximum of 100 points based on the following criteria:

  1. Technique: 30 points max
  2. Choreography: 30 points max
  3. Presentation: 20 points max
  4. Musicality: 20 points max

Using the Olympic scoring system were one highest and one lowest scores are eliminated, the points awarded for each dance will be the aggregate of the remaining three scores.

Winning Performances (1, 2 and 3 places) awarded with Diplomas and Trophies

Runners-up (4, 5 and 6 places) awarded with Diplomas and Large Medals

Performances are scored within their own declared nomination: Age Group and Category

DAY 1 (Europestage Kids Day) / 3–9 years old – places can be duplicated in each Age Group and Category.

DAY 2 / 10+ years old – first three places only (i.e. 1st, 2nd and 3rd) DO NOT GET DUPLICATED within the same Age Group and Category, except for:

  • In case of a tie (that is when the same number of points are awarded)
  • In case of 1 point separating places (working from top going down)

CASH or CASH EQUIVALENT PRIZES

The top scoring performance (with the highest number of points out of all categories) IN EACH AGE GROUP will receive a €200 (two hundred euros) cash prize. In case of a tie, it will be up to the Judges to determine the winner.

DAY 1 (Europestage Kids Day) / 3–9 years old – the top scoring performance choreographer of the day will receive an all-inclusive pre-booked (by Europestage) trip to the “Camp Fabrika” week in Istanbul (flight, accommodation and workshops), up to a maximum value of €1,000

DAY 2 / 10+ years old – the top scoring performance choreographer of the day will receive an all-inclusive pre-booked (by Europestage) trip to the “Camp Fabrika” week in Istanbul (flight, accommodation and workshops), up to a maximum value of €1,000.
